Commercial Locksmith Services

Services designed for shops, offices, warehouses and multi-tenant buildings — from urgent entry to planned access-system rollouts.

Typical scenarios

  • After-hours lockouts for retail or offices.
  • Broken cylinders or multi-point failures on high-usage doors.
  • Tenant changes requiring rekeys or master-key adjustments.
  • New site installs for access control or panic hardware.

Our approach

We prioritise non-destructive entry where possible, then assess whether repair or full replacement is the most cost‑effective and secure option. For system work we provide site surveys, a written specification and phased installation plans to minimise business disruption.

What we deliver

  • Emergency and scheduled entry with identity checks and documentation.
  • Cylinder and mortice lock repairs or supply of heavy‑duty replacements.
  • Supply and integration of access control (keypad/fob/remote) with user management.
  • Design and implementation of master key suites with restricted keying.
  • Panic bar installation, testing and compliance adjustments.
  • Replacement door furniture, closers and reinforced strikes for heavy-use entrances.
  • Fast on-site key cutting and temporary keyed-alike solutions while permanent work is scheduled.
  • Service-level agreements and planned maintenance packages for multi-site clients.

Timeframe & parts

Typical on-site repairs: 30–90 minutes. System surveys and quoting: 1–3 business days. We stock common commercial cylinders, high-security cores and panic bar kits for fast replacement.

Recommendations

For high-traffic doors choose commercial-grade hardware and schedule regular maintenance. We can set up quarterly or annual checks to reduce sudden failures and keep warranties valid.

FAQ

Will you force a commercial door to gain entry?
No — we attempt non-destructive entry first and explain options and costs before any forced work.
Do you work outside normal hours?
Yes — we offer 24/7 emergency attendance for commercial lockouts and urgent security issues.
Can you integrate with our existing access control?
Yes — we survey systems and provide integration or replacement plans with a written specification.

Residential Locksmith Services

Help for homeowners, landlords and tenants — rapid access, smart lock installs, and security upgrades tailored to domestic needs.

Common situations

  • Locked out of house or flat (non-destructive entry preferred).
  • Lost or stolen keys requiring immediate rekey or lock change.
  • Upgrading ageing cylinders to anti-snap / anti-bump standards.
  • Smart lock installation for keyless access and remote control.

Step-by-step service

We confirm identity where required, explain entry options and only proceed with your approval. For replacements we discuss cylinder grades, demonstrate the new hardware and complete a functional test before leaving.

Parts & expected times

Simple entries: 20–45 minutes. Cylinder replacement: 30–60 minutes. Smart lock installs: 45–120 minutes depending on wiring. We fit anti-snap cylinders, reinforced strike plates and tidy finishes.

Advice

For rental properties schedule rekeys between tenancies and consider restricted keying for multi-property managers. Combine cylinder upgrades with strike/hinge reinforcement for best protection.

FAQ

How quickly can you get me back in?
Typical non-destructive entries take 20–45 minutes; we prioritise urgent calls.
Will a smart lock work on my door?
We check mechanical compatibility first — multipoint gearboxes or poor alignment can cause failures; we advise before installing.
Do upgrades meet insurance requirements?
We recommend and fit insurance‑approved cylinders and provide documentation for claims.

Additional residential services we commonly provide include window and patio lock repairs, installation of keyed-alike sets for convenience, and simple child-safety lock options.

Emergency Locksmith Services

24/7 urgent help for lockouts and sudden security problems. We aim for non-destructive entry first and confirm pricing before any work begins.

Typical emergency calls

  • Home and apartment lockouts.
  • Car lockouts (most modern vehicles).
  • Broken key extraction and door re-entry.
  • Lock failure: jammed, seized, or misaligned hardware.
  • Post-break-in re-securing and temporary fixes.

What to expect

  • Quick triage on the phone (door type, lock type, urgency).
  • Arrival window and confirmation of on-site assessment.
  • Entry method explained on site, with options and a clear estimate. We only proceed once you approve.
  • If replacement parts are required, we can fit common cylinders and hardware on the spot.

Non-destructive first

We prioritise techniques like picking, bypassing, decoding and careful hardware adjustments. If drilling or forced entry becomes necessary, we explain why, confirm the cost, and discuss immediate replacement options.

After-hours notes

After-hours and emergency rates may apply depending on the time and complexity. Final pricing is confirmed before work starts.

FAQ

Do you offer 24/7 emergency locksmith service?
Yes. We’re available around the clock for urgent lockouts and security issues.
Will you damage my door or lock?
We attempt non-destructive entry first. If a destructive method is required, we explain options and costs before proceeding.
Can you open modern cars?
In many cases, yes. We’ll confirm the vehicle type on the phone and advise the safest approach.

Storefront Door Repair

Rapid repairs and durable replacements to keep shops and retail premises open and secure with minimal disruption to trade.

Typical problems

  • Doors sticking or failing to engage multi-point locks after settling.
  • Panic bar failure or misalignment preventing safe egress.
  • Glass/door damage from forced entry needing immediate boarding.

Our process

We triage on arrival: secure the premises if needed, then decide between alignment/repair or full hardware replacement. For businesses we can provide temporary boarding and schedule permanent works at a convenient time.

Typical fixes & timing

  • Door realignment and closer adjustment: 30–90 minutes.
  • Panic bar repair or replacement: 30–120 minutes depending on door type.
  • Boarding & temporary security: immediate, followed by permanent repair within an agreed timescale.

We can also fit auto-door mechanisms and specialist hinges for very high-traffic entrances, and provide staggered repair plans to minimise trading interruptions.

Customer notes

If a forced entry has occurred we recommend documenting damage for insurers and arranging secure boarding immediately; we can assist with photos and a written handover.

FAQ

Can you board a shop and allow trading?
Yes — we provide neat temporary boarding and can implement a short-term fix to allow trading until permanent repairs are carried out.
How fast can you repair a panic bar?
Minor adjustments or latch replacements are often same-day; full replacements depend on door type and parts availability.
Do you help with insurance paperwork?
We supply photos and a short report to support claims.

Property Eviction

Controlled, lawful re-securing and lock changes after evictions or tenancy terminations — focused on safety and clear handover.

What we handle

  • Immediate boarding and securing to prevent unauthorised re-entry.
  • Full external lock replacement and internal rekeys as requested.
  • Documented key handover with tamper-evident labelling where required.
  • Coordination with property managers, agents or authorised officers.

Procedure

We follow a clear procedure: verification of authorised instructions, immediate securing (if necessary), then planned lock changes. We provide written notes and photos for records and can supply new keys sealed in labelled packs for the client or agent.

Timing & records

Emergency securing and boarding: immediate. Full rekey or replacement depending on property size: 1–3 hours. We supply a brief report documenting actions taken, parts used and keys handed over.

Additional eviction services include mailbox and gate lock changes, padlock replacements for yards, and secure labelling of delivered key sets for audit purposes.

Safe Opening

Specialist safe services emphasising non-destructive methods first, then careful repair or managed replacement when necessary.

Approach & options

  • Non-destructive entry using specialist tools and techniques where safe design allows.

  • FAQ

    Do you need written authorisation?
    Yes — we act only on verified instructions from authorised agents or owners and record the chain of custody.
    How are keys delivered after rekeying?
    We label and seal key sets with client details and provide a short handover certificate.
    Can you change mailbox/gate locks too?
    Yes — we offer a full perimeter re‑securing service.
    Combination changes, lock unit replacements and servicing of internal mechanisms.
  • Destructive entry only with client consent, followed by repair or replacement recommendations.

What to expect

We inspect the safe and advise on likely outcomes and cost before attempting work. Many domestic safes can be opened without drilling; larger or bespoke units sometimes require graduated techniques. We always document the process and offer repair or replacement options afterwards.

Timing

Minor non-destructive jobs: 1–3 hours. Complex or destructive openings: may require a full day and follow-up repairs. We carry common replacement lock units and parts for many manufacturers.

FAQ

Can my safe be opened without drilling?
Often — many domestic safes are opened non-destructively; we advise after inspection.
Do you handle contents securely?
Yes — we use tamper-evident bags and provide a signed checklist for any items handled.
How long will repairs take after opening?
Small repairs same-day; complex repairs or parts orders may require follow-up visits.

We also offer combination-recovery services, secure handling of high-value item inventories and liaison with insurers when required for claims handling.

Push Bar / Panic Bar

Supply, install and maintain certified exit devices to ensure safe, reliable egress and compliance with fire-safety guidance.

Services provided

  • Supply and professional installation of compliant panic bars for single and double doors.
  • On-site repair, adjustment and latch replacement for smooth operation.
  • Full compliance checks and advice on meeting local regulations and accessibility requirements.

Inspection & maintenance

We test release force, door alignment and panic-bar operation. Regular maintenance prevents failures and ensures egress remains reliable; we offer scheduled inspections and rapid repair visits if a device is reported faulty.

Installation notes

Correct fitting often requires door reinforcement or astragal adjustments; we assess the door and recommend the right device (rim, vertical rod or surface-mounted) for durability and compliance.

FAQ

How often should panic bars be tested?
We recommend annual inspections for high-use doors and immediate checks after any reported fault.
Can you upgrade existing bars to meet codes?
Yes — we assess current fittings and recommend compliant replacements where necessary.
Do you provide certification?
We can issue a simple test certificate and keep records for audits on request.

We keep common spare parts and can convert devices between rim and vertical-rod styles if a building layout changes, helping to future-proof installations.
