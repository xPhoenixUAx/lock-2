Property Eviction

Controlled, lawful re-securing and lock changes after evictions or tenancy terminations — focused on safety and clear handover.

What we handle

  • Immediate boarding and securing to prevent unauthorised re-entry.
  • Full external lock replacement and internal rekeys as requested.
  • Documented key handover with tamper-evident labelling where required.
  • Coordination with property managers, agents or authorised officers.

Procedure

We follow a clear procedure: verification of authorised instructions, immediate securing (if necessary), then planned lock changes. We provide written notes and photos for records and can supply new keys sealed in labelled packs for the client or agent.

Timing & records

Emergency securing and boarding: immediate. Full rekey or replacement depending on property size: 1–3 hours. We supply a brief report documenting actions taken, parts used and keys handed over.

Additional eviction services include mailbox and gate lock changes, padlock replacements for yards, and secure labelling of delivered key sets for audit purposes.

Property handover

Documented, controlled re-securing.

Clear authorisation, careful work, and tidy records — designed for landlords, agents, and property managers.

  • Verification of instructions before starting
  • Immediate securing and temporary boarding (if needed)
  • Lock changes, rekeys, and perimeter updates
  • Labelled key handover for audit trails
  • Notes/photos available for your records

What to provide

Property address, authorised contact details, and any specific lock/key requirements. We can coordinate pickup/handovers with agents.
