Commercial Locksmith Services

Services designed for shops, offices, warehouses and multi-tenant buildings — from urgent entry to planned access-system rollouts.

Typical scenarios

  • After-hours lockouts for retail or offices.
  • Broken cylinders or multi-point failures on high-usage doors.
  • Tenant changes requiring rekeys or master-key adjustments.
  • New site installs for access control or panic hardware.

Our approach

We prioritise non-destructive entry where possible, then assess whether repair or full replacement is the most cost-effective and secure option. For system work we provide site surveys, a written specification and phased installation plans to minimise business disruption.

What we deliver

  • Emergency and scheduled entry with identity checks and documentation.
  • Cylinder and mortice lock repairs or supply of heavy-duty replacements.
  • Supply and integration of access control (keypad/fob/remote) with user management.
  • Design and implementation of master key suites with restricted keying.
  • Panic bar installation, testing and compliance adjustments.
  • Replacement door furniture, closers and reinforced strikes for heavy-use entrances.
  • Fast on-site key cutting and temporary keyed-alike solutions while permanent work is scheduled.
  • Service-level agreements and planned maintenance packages for multi-site clients.

Timeframe & parts

Typical on-site repairs: 30–90 minutes. System surveys and quoting: 1–3 business days. We stock common commercial cylinders, high-security cores and panic bar kits for fast replacement.

Recommendations

For high-traffic doors choose commercial-grade hardware and schedule regular maintenance. We can set up quarterly or annual checks to reduce sudden failures and keep warranties valid.

FAQ

Will you force a commercial door to gain entry?
No — we attempt non-destructive entry first and explain options and costs before any forced work.
Do you work outside normal hours?
Yes — we offer 24/7 emergency attendance for commercial lockouts and urgent security issues.
Can you integrate with our existing access control?
Yes — we survey systems and provide integration or replacement plans with a written specification.
