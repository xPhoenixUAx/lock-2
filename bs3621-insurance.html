BS3621 explained in plain English

BS3621 is a recognized security standard used by many insurers. We supply and fit BS3621-rated locks for added protection.

Why it matters

BS3621 locks are known for durability and resistance to common forced-entry methods. Many insurers recognize the standard as a strong option for homes and rental properties.

  • Recognized security standard option
  • Improved resistance to drilling and picking
  • Helps meet insurer expectations
