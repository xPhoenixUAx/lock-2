A simple commercial security checklist

Jan 17, 2026 • 4 min read

Most commercial lock issues can be prevented with a quick monthly walkthrough. Here’s a simple checklist you can use for offices, storefronts, and multi-tenant buildings.

Door operation

  • Does the door close smoothly without slamming?
  • Does the latch engage fully every time?
  • Any scraping at the threshold or frame?

Hardware and closers

  • Any loose screws, handles, or wobble in the lock case?
  • Is the closer leaking fluid or losing tension?
  • Are strikes reinforced and aligned?

Key control

  • Do you know who has keys right now?
  • Any recent staff changes that require rekeying?
  • Consider a master key plan for clean access control.

Exit devices

  • Do panic bars release smoothly without sticking?
  • Are doors aligned so egress is reliable every time?
  • Schedule regular inspections for high-traffic entrances.

If any item above fails, it’s usually cheaper to fix early than to deal with an after-hours lockout or a door that won’t secure.
